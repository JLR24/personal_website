Musician's Haven

Date Started: 01/11/2021 State: In Progress
Date Finished: DD/MM/YYYY Team Members: Jack Ricketts

This project was initially a school project that I completed when I was in sixth form. This was a mandatory project called the EPQ. I completed this first version of the project in the summer of 2022 (as per the EPQ deadline), however I decided that I wanted to redo it to a much higher standard with a focus on improving design and usability, as well as adding new features.

The website itself can be summarised as "a source of music information crossed with social media for musician's". It contains features such as information about songs/bands, forum threads, profiles (which users can interact with through posting and following), instant messaging, free music courses, band pages (which allow users to find bandmates meeting their criteria), and music recommendations.

Personal Website

Date Started: 21/01/2024 State: Complete
Date Finished: DD/01/2024 Team Members: Jack Ricketts

Although this website is not very advanced or technically-challenging, the project had a strong focus on front-end design and completeness, since it would be the first project of mine to be made publicly available. The website contents are very simple: a home page; a page to display my projects; and an "about" page, which includes a contact form. The site is made using a simple Flask application.
