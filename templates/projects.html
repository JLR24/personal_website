Musician's Haven

Date Started: 01/11/2021 State: In Progress
Date Finished: DD/MM/YYYY Team Members: Jack Ricketts

This project was initially a school project that I completed when I was in sixth form. This was a mandatory project called the EPQ. I completed this first version of the project in the summer of 2022 (as per the EPQ deadline). However, I decided that I wanted to recreate the website to a much higher standard with a focus on improving design and usability, as well as adding some new features.

The website itself can be summarised as a source of music information crossed with social media for musicians. It contains features such as information about songs and bands, forum threads, profiles (which users can interact with through posting and following), instant messaging, free music courses, band pages (which allow users to find bandmates meeting their criteria), and music recommendations.

The site's backend makes use of Python and Flask and the frontend uses HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. Sockets were used for the direct messaging feature.

Warwick Bakery

Date Started: 17/03/2024 State: In Progress
Date Finished: DD/04/2024 Team Members: Jack Ricketts, Warwick Bakery

A friend started a bakery business while at university, and asked me to create a website for them. The site allows customers to view products and make orders. Staff members can then process these orders accordingly. In addition, the site also contains the company's books. I am responsible for maintaining the completed website.

Since this website is not for just my own personal use, I had to ensure that every feature worked exactly as intended, could not be exploited, and was accessible to other people. To do this, I conducted thorough testing and did extra research into security and common defects.
